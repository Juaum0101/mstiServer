The MSK Mutation Formula (The Mold)

1. Header Information

Name: The unique title of the mutation.

Grade: Categorizes the power and cost level:

Alpha: High power or high versatility.

Beta: Specific utility or niche mechanics.

Omega: Purely stat-focused or basic mechanical shifts.

Type/Theme: The power source (e.g., Telekinesis, Elemental, Evo Natural).

2. The Vital Sacrifice (HP Costs)

Base Mutation Tax: A flat -5 Max HP penalty for having any mutation at all.

Grade Penalty:

Alpha: -6 Max HP.

Beta: -2 Max HP.

Omega: (Usually carries a smaller or specific penalty depending on the stat buff).

Total HP Reduction: The sum of the tax and the grade penalty.

3. The "One Thing" Power

Passive Mechanics: Constant effects that alter how the player interacts with the game (e.g., "Ignore self-inflicted Burning," "Sense opponent stamina").

Active Mechanics: Enables specific types of techniques (e.g., "Allows you to use telekinetic techniques").

Weakness Mitigation: Does this mutation cover a hole in a specific archetype's design?

4. Mutation Techniques (Fixed Actions)

Fixed Cards: List any techniques added to the character's arsenal that are always available and do not count toward the Active Memory limit.
